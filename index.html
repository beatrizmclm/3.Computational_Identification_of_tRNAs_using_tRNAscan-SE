Part A: Manual creation of an anticodon
a. What is anticodon?
The anticodon is a sequence of three nucleotide base pairs in the tRNA that code for one amino acid
and binds to the complimentary mRNA during protein synthesis in the ribosome.
b. What are the roles of anticodons
The anticodons bind to the complimentary mRNA and its role is to bring the correct amino acid to
create a protein.
c. How do you manually create anticodons?
We can translate a sequence of 3 nucleotides from the mRNA to create the anticodon that is
complimentary to the mRNA codon.
